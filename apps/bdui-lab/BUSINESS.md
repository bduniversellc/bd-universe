# Business Specification

Application: BDUI Lab

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Development

---

# Purpose

BDUI Lab is the engineering workspace for the BD Universe Design System.

It enables rapid development, testing, and documentation of reusable components before they become part of production applications.

---

# Business Value

- Faster component development
- Higher UI consistency
- Reduced duplication
- Improved quality assurance
- Easier onboarding for contributors

---

# Primary Users

- Designers
- Front-end Developers
- Product Engineers
- QA Engineers

---

# Success Criteria

Every reusable BDUI component should have:

- Documentation
- Live Preview
- Contract
- Version History
- Example Usage

before being used by an application.
