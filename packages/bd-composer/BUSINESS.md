# Business Specification

Subsystem

BD Composer

# Purpose

BD Composer transforms reusable engineering assets into deployable applications.

It eliminates duplicated layouts and enables every BD Universe brand to share the same architectural foundation.

---

# Business Value

- Faster development
- Lower maintenance costs
- Higher component reuse
- Consistent quality
- Easier onboarding
- Scalable architecture
