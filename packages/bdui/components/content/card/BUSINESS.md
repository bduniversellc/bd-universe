# Business Specification

Component: Card

Version: 1.0.0

---

## Purpose

Cards organize information into reusable visual units.

Instead of creating custom layouts for every project, the Card component creates consistency across the entire ecosystem.

---

## Business Value

- Faster development
- Better readability
- Consistent branding
- Easier maintenance
- Higher component reuse

---

## Success

Every Blueprint should use the Card component.
