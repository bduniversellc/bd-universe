# Business Specification

Component: Hero

Version: 1.0.0

---

## Purpose

The Hero creates the first impression.

A visitor should understand who we are, what we do, and why it matters in less than 10 seconds.

---

## Business Goal

Increase engagement by communicating trust, clarity, and purpose immediately.

---

## Success Metrics

- Visitor engagement
- CTA clicks
- Scroll depth
- Bounce rate

---

## Guiding Principle

Clarity before creativity.
