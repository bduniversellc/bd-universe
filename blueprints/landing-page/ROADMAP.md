# Vision

Blueprint 001 is the reference implementation of a modern business landing page built entirely with BDUI components.

It serves as the foundation for future BD Universe websites and demonstrates the engineering principles of BDOS, BDDS, and BDUI.

---

# Mission

Build a reusable, accessible, responsive, and AI-ready landing page that can be customized for any founder, startup, agency, or business without rebuilding the architecture.
