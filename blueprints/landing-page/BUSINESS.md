Name: Landing Page

Version: 1.0.0

---

# Executive Summary

Blueprint 001 is the standard landing page architecture for the BD Universe platform.

It combines engineering, branding, accessibility, and reusable components into a production-ready website foundation that can be adapted for multiple industries.

Rather than designing every website from scratch, Blueprint 001 provides a repeatable system that delivers consistent quality, faster development, and long-term maintainability.

---

# Business Purpose

Blueprint 001 exists to reduce the time, cost, and complexity of building professional business websites.

Its purpose is to transform website development from a custom design process into an engineering process.
